Activity:
Rest at home for the remainder of today. You received sedation and may feel sleepy or dizzy. You must not drive a car, operate any machinery, or make any important financial or legal decisions for 24 hours. Strenuous exercise should be avoided for 48 hours.
Diet:
Begin with clear liquids such as water, broth, or ginger ale for the next 4 hours. 2. A small amount of blood (a few streaks on toilet paper) might be present with your first one or two bowel movements. This is not unusual.
