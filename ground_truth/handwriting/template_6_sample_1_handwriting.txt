Microscopic Description: Microscopic examination reveals an exophytic proliferation of benign basaloid keratinocytes. No evidence of dysplasia or malignancy is identified.
Diagnosis: Skin, left cheek, shave biopsy: Seborrheic keratosis, irritated.
